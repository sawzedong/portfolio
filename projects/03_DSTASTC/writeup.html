Introduction to the Camp

Under the Young Defence Scientists Programme (YDSP), DSTA hosts a YDSP Science and Technology Camp every year. In 2021, I participated in this camp in a team of six students from my school. The theme of the camp was "Into the World of Virtual and Augmented Reality".

My Experience

Through sharings by professors as well as dialogue sessions, there were many opportunities to learn more about what Virtual Reality/Augmented Reality (VR/AR) was and how it has been growing in the past few years. These industry experts shared about its applications in the real-world context, particularly in the context of national total defence.

The sharings also included opportunities for us to try working on VR/AR in a hands-on manner. Various trainers taught us the basics of using platforms such as CoSpaces (now Delightex), as well as the programming needed to create a functional VR/AR experience.

The Hackathon

However, the main highlight was the hackathon where we had to use CoSpaces to create a simulation of VR for a real-world application. We identified that while existing simulations do exist for terrorism scenario training, these simulations are often ran on computers and can provide unrealistic backgrounds for these servicepeople.

Terrorist attacks can happen in many different ways and environments. Hence, our team designed a Prototype VR game to Simulate Terrorist Attacks in Singapore. This was a way to help servicepeople train under simulated conditions meant to emulate actual scenarios, preparing them mentally and physically for such events.

Reflections

Through the sharings, I was able to better appreciate how such new technologies, such as VR and AR, that may appear to only be useful for entertainment, can have many different uses in real life. Through my research and the sharings, I began to get a view into how such novel technologies like VR have many applications even in vital installations, such as in helping military training. Overall, it was a fun learning experience while working with my friends on coming up with and working on a strong pitch for our idea.
