Introduction to Student Council

The Student Council's (SC) main objective is to plan initiatives, events and other projects for the school. In 2020, I was fortunate to be selected to take part in an one-year internship in the student council (not as an elected member). I was part of the Student Engagement department, and was also part of the Orientation project to welcome the students that were newly entering Dunman High School.

Orientation: Publicity

I was a part of the publicity team for Orientation. Throughout the planning process, we had a large number of meetings to coordinate games and produce videos. I also took the opportunity to learn more about photography and video editing, putting together special effects videos, montages and publicity ideas. Although the process was long and took time, I still found the experience very rewarding when looking at the smile on the faces of the new students after orientation.

Student Engagement (SE)

During my internship, apart from the project, I was also attached to one of the departments within Student Council. SE primarily focused on smaller initiatives and quality-of-life changes, addressing the day-to-day needs of the student body. During my time in SE, we initiated focus group discussions and reached out to the various representatives of the student body to better understand the needs of the student population.

Reflections

In 2021, in order to take on the role of Infocomm CCA Chairperson, I opted to not continue serving in SC.

However, the time that I was able to help give back to the school and to be in SC was a very fruitful experience. Through my interactions with my seniors and my experiences of watching them lead the committes I was in, I observed many leadership traits, styles, and qualities. One big takeaway I had was the importance of people-centric leadership; as many teams will often comprise of people of different strengths and personalities, I understood the importance of tapping on the value of every single member.
