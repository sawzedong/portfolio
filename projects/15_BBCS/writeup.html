Introduction to BuildingBloCS

BuildingBloCS (BBCS) is a student-led organisation dedicated to year-long computing outreach. It aims to make computing more accessible to students, while deepening the skills of those with existing knowledge. Hence, it hosts conferences almost every quarterly, combining workshops and a hackathon.

I joined BBCS in 2023, and began with the planning for the June conference held at NUS. Subsequently, I also helped to plan the September 2023 (virtual), March 2024 (virtual), and June 2024 (held at SMU) conferences as well. During this team, I took on multiple different roles, allowing me to gain a wealth of experience in these aspects.

BBCS: General Organiser

As an organiser, I helped with administrative matters for the conferences. For June 2023, I planned the booth layouts for the industry booths and the hackathon groups. I was also the group facilitator for the June 2023 and 2024 conferences. As the groups comprised members of different schools, it was important for us to help them break the ice and bond as they would be working with each other for the hackathon.

BBCS: Workshop Speaker

I was a workshop speaker at all the conferences that I planned. The workshops I spoke at were as follows: "The AI of the Beholder: Seeing with Computer Vision" (an introduction to Python OpenCV) "Intro to HTML/CSS/JS" "Data Visualisation with Matplotlib and Seaborn" "Introduction and Extension of Scikit-learn"

As a workshop speaker, I guided beginners in understanding the principles of the topic and how they could practically apply it themselves. A key lesson I picked up was how to contextualise the presentaiton and delivery to my target audience. Hence, I dedicated more effort to making analogies, diagrams, and clear slides for them to grasp it more easily. These workshops gave me a stronger sense of confidence, and greater understanding of how to conduct such sharings/workshops at various scales.

BBCS: EXCO Member (Sept 2023)

I was a member of the Executive Committee of BBCS for the Sept 2023 conference, held over virtual meetings. As EXCO, I helped to coordinate the overall structure and idea of the conference. We planned the theme, timings of workshops and ensured alignment of the various departments.

BBCS: Lead of Outreach Team (2024)

In 2024, I also served as one of two leaders of the outreach team. We led our members in contacting teachers to recruit participants, and contacting companies to recruit sponsors. Both of these were key stakeholders in the success of our conferences. Hence, we ensured extensive outreach to establish strong links to relevant parties.

Reflections

I thoroughly enjoyed the variety of experiences I had while I was here. From interacting and playing games with all the participants, to conducting a workshop for the first time, to planning such a large-scale event, to running around on the day itself, to the organiser gatherings for workshop dry runs or cohesion, it was fun while also serving a meaningful purpose. It really added joy to my holidays!

While it was mostly an opportunity for me to use my skills and knowledge to benefit the community, I also grew in both being a team player to leading (in terms of facilitation, workshop, or even my outreach team). The various contexts challenged my priorities, and I learnt how to simultaneously manage the people and the task at hand. It also strengthened my technical skills, as I sought mastery before I taught the content. Overall, I am grateful for the experience and to everyone who was involved in it.

Disclaimer: While these works were done more recently, I do recognise there are still areas for improvement. However, they remain available for reference and also as opportunities for future works!
